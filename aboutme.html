Zeid Jaouni is currently completing a BFA in Communication Design at Parsons School of Design in New York, NY, with an interest in the fashion industry, working with branding, print, editorials, and other such jobs. He is Passionate, respectful, and a creative person with intact leadership skills and capabilities who has a drive for improvement, success, and ethical decision making. He also has an interest in modeling, wanting to gain as much experience and success as possible.
He always likes to experiment with different art forms such as photography, film, sculptures, paintings, drawings, digital work, and many more to expand his knowledge and experience in many areas/fields.
He has been involved in many publications such as GQ Middle East, Bloomingdales Middle East, and Harvey Nichols Dubai.
